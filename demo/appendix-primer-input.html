APPENDIX: PRIMER INPUT

One worksheet holds every table your model needs.

Primer populates a single tab called Primer Input. Your formulas reference it. The sheet grows to hold whatever your deal requires.
Rent Roll
Every unit, status, type, square footage, and in-place rent.
T-12 / P&L
Trailing income and expenses, with the seller's GL codes mapped to yours.
Unit Mix
Counts and average rents by bedroom and bath configuration.
Rent Comps
Market rents from comparable properties, fetched from configured sources.
Sales Comps
Recent sale prices, cap rates, and price-per-unit for comparable assets.
Property Facts
Year built, units, address, lot size, and other site-level details.
Tax and Assessor Data
Current assessed value, tax bills, and parcel details, fetched from public records.
Your Custom Fields
Any metric, score, or flag your team needs, added during setup.
Every cell on this sheet is cited to a source document, a page, or a public record. Nothing is inferred without a footnote.
