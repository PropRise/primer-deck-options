APPENDIX: WHAT ELSE PRIMER AUTOMATES

The rent roll and the P&L are just the start.

Primer reads every section of the deal package and fills your model from end to end. Here are six areas most teams still do by hand.
T-12 Normalization
Annualizes partial-year line items and adjusts for one-time items before they land in your model. The Jasmine T-12 ran only 10 months; Primer grossed it up automatically.
Operating Expense Detail
Breaks out each expense category from the T-12, maps seller GL codes to your chart of accounts, and flags lines that are missing or lumped together, such as insurance rolled into admin.
Debt Service and Loan Terms
Extracts rate, amortization, IO period, maturity, and prepayment terms from loan documents or the OM and maps them to your debt assumptions tab.
Lease and Tenant Rollover
Reads lease expirations, renewal options, and concession schedules from tenant files and populates your rollover matrix so you can see vacancy exposure by year.
Renovation and CapEx Budget
Pulls unit renovation scope, cost per unit, and schedule from the capital plan or OM, then fills renovation cost, stabilized unit count, and timeline into your model.
Real Estate Tax and Assessor Data
Fetches the current assessed value, tax bill, and exemption status directly from the county assessor website and cross-checks it against what the T-12 reports.
Every field Primer fills is cited back to the source document, page, and table. Fields it cannot find are flagged, never estimated.
