WHAT PRIMER IS

An acquisitions platform that runs your
first pass in your own model.

Forward a deal to Primer or drop it in the app. It reads the entire deal room, runs the market and tax research, and populates your existing Excel model — a citation on every cell. The first-pass model population is what Primer automates today; your assumptions and judgment stay yours.

Runs in your model

Primer writes one Primer Input worksheet, then maps it into your tabs, formulas, and chart of accounts. Nothing to rebuild or learn.
